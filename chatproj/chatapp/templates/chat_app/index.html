Frequently Asked Questions

Any queries you have may be found here.

  • How does the returns process work?

    It's easy! In your package, there will be a returns label. Attach this to your return post and send the parcel to the address given.

  • Shoes show in stock when not in stock and we can still proceed to checkout. What do I do?

    We are trying to resolve this issue as soon as possible. If you see this, please report this to us through the Contact Us Form, or call us or use the online chat.

  • What are the delivery dates/time?

    Standard home deliveries are usually made within 2 - 3 days of your order being placed. Next Day deliveries will be made the next day if your order has been placed before 9pm. Please note the exact delivery time can vary depending on the service selected at checkout.
